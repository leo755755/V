About The Author

Dylan

Dylan is an upcoming and budding author. Dylan worked as a journalist for 5 years before turning his hand into fiction. His speciality lies in fantasy, murder mystery and psychological thrillers. He is the best selling author of "The Boy at the cafe" that was launched in 2010. His upcoming novel "V" is a psychological thriller and murder mystery novel which will enthrall you till the very end.
